How To Buy?

Step 1
Connect Wallet

Find the presale widget at the top of this web page and connect your preferred wallet. There are various options to choose from.

Step 2
Select Network

Once connected, you can choose to participate in the Dogeverse presale on Ethereum, BNB Chain, Polygon, Avalanche or Base. Solana is also coming soon!

Step 3
Buy Tokens

Enter the amount of ETH, BNB, MATIC, AVAX, USDT or USDC you wish to swap for $DOGEVERSE and confirm the transaction in your wallet.

Step 4
Claim on Chain

Once the presale concludes, you will be able to claim your $DOGEVERSE tokens using the same wallet and chain you used to buy.

Step 5
Bridge or Trade

$DOGEVERSE will be tradable on all major chains and DEXs. Simply use the Dogeverse Bridge to easily switch tokens between chains.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Dogeverse?
Dogeverse is the first truly multichain meme coin, with Cosmo the Doge bringing $DOGEVERSE to all the biggest blockchains.
How do I participate in the presale?
You can take part in the presale on Ethereum, Polygon, or BNB Chain. All you need to do is connect your wallet to the presale widget above and send ETH, BNB, MATIC, or USDT to reserve $DOGEVERSE tokens.
When do I receive my presale tokens?
Once you've completed your presale purchase, you'll be able to use the same wallet to claim your tokens before the first DEX listing. Claim and listing dates will be announced on our social media accounts.
What is Dogeverse staking?
Staking is the action of sending your tokens to a smart contract and locking them for a defined period of time. This will earn you more $DOGEVERSE tokens over time at a dynamic rate. Dogeverse staking is only available on the Ethereum network for now. For more information, click on 'Staking' in the navigation bar.
Is there a way to contact support?
We're here to help! Kindly join our Telegram channel to find prompt support from a Dogeverse moderator. You could also email and await a response.
